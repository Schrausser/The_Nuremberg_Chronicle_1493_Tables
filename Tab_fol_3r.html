De opera secunde diei.

" Fecit e[ni]m deus firmamentum rotundum mobile cetera sensibilia continens.", (Schedel, 1493, fol. IIIr).

" Got hat das firmament gescheibelt. beweglich. andere empfintliche dig begreiffende gemacht.", (Schedel and Alt, 1493, 1496, bl. IIIr).

"God divided the firmament and made it movable; and he made other sensitive things comprehensible.", (Hadavas, 2023, p. 4).
