¹¹) The ***Edict of Milan*** (313), Constantine I (272-337) granted equal status of *Christianity* with the Roman religion.

¹⁵) ***Restauratio imperii***, the *Restoration of the Empire* (555) under Justinian I (482-565).  
¹⁶) The ***Hijrah*** (622), *beginning* of the Islamic calendar under Muhammad (c. 570-632) and of the *Muslim conquests*.  
¹⁷) ***Translatio imperii***, the *transfer* of rule under Charlemagne (748-814), he united Western Europe and was *first* emperor (800) *after the fall* of West Rome (476).
